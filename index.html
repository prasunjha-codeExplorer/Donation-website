Actual meaning of donation!

A donation simply means giving something—like money, goods, time, or even a part of yourself such as blood or organs—freely and willingly, without expecting anything in return. It comes from a sense of generosity or compassion, where the intention is to help someone, support a cause, or contribute to the welfare of others.

Help Stray Dogs

Helping a stray dog brings a kind of happiness that’s pure and unspoken. Unlike people, dogs don’t use words to thank you—they show it through their eyes, their wagging tails, or sometimes just the calmness they feel in your presence. When you give food, water, or even a gentle touch to a stray, you can almost see their fear slowly turn into trust. you realize you’ve given safety and kindness to a soul that has probably known more struggle than comfort.

About Thaggam

Thagam Community

Thaagam Foundation is a registered Section 8 company (under the Companies Act, 2013), functioning as a non-profit organization headquartered at No.6, Dhanammal Street, Spurtank Road, Chetpet, Chennai—registration number U80220TN2018NPL124811 globalhindufederation.net NGO Details . The foundation is driven by a mission to combat hunger and uplift underprivileged communities through holistic initiatives in food distribution, education, healthcare, environmental protection, and animal welfare globalhindufederation.net thaagam.foundation Thaagam Foundation is a Chennai-based nonprofit actively addressing hunger, education, healthcare, animal welfare, and environmental issues. They’ve demonstrated strong momentum—serving millions of meals and engaging hundreds of thousands of donors—with a commitment to transparency and documentation. While overall reports are positive, some skepticism still exists online. If you're considering involvement, it might help to review their annual reports or reach out directly to assess their operations further.



What we did so far

  • Has served over 11.5 million meals to the homeless and underprivileged, including variety rice meals like tomato rice, lemon rice, and puliyogare

  • Since December 2021, they've run a Child Education Program in Delhi and Noida, providing tuition fees, school supplies, and educational materials.

  • Launched "Crowdfund Education" in April 2024, connecting donors directly to students needing financial aid.

  • During the Sri Lankan economic crisis, they distributed grocery kits worth ₹10 lakhs over 18 days and established an office in Colombo with government approval to continue relief efforts
